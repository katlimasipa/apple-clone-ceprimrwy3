Questions? Answers.

Is Aura compatible with non-native devices?

+

Yes. While Aura Pro offers an enhanced experience with native ecosystems, it connects via Bluetooth 5.3 to any compatible smartphone, tablet, or computer.

How does the spatial audio tracking work?

+

Using built-in gyroscopes and accelerometers, Aura tracks the movement of your head relative to your device, remapping the sound field so it stays anchored to your screen.

What is the warranty coverage?

+

Aura comes with a standard one-year limited warranty and up to 90 days of complimentary technical support. Protection plans are available at checkout.
